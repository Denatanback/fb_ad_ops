# Workflows

## Creative lifecycle
1. Creative is added to the library
2. Creative enters queue
3. Creative moves to active
4. Creative is stopped or moved to scaling
5. Creative may receive tags:
   - winner
   - loser
   - top_ctr

## Launch lifecycle
1. Launch is created from a Creative
2. Launch is assigned a Lander and setup
3. Metrics are entered
4. Launch outcome influences Creative evaluation

## Core analytical views
- Launch level
- Creative level
- Approach level
